HOW THE INTELLIGENCE PIPELINE WORKS
1. Research Desk scrapes Reuters, AP, ESPN, and Reddit in real time. Keywords are matched against 30+ active Polymarket contracts.
2. Math Desk evaluates implied probability vs order book pricing. Checks ΣPᵢ = 1.00 boundary. Flags sum violations and Kelly edge > 0.5%.
3. Execution Desk (Claude AI) reviews all findings. If conviction exceeds your threshold, it calls buy_yes or buy_no with size + price.
4. Chief Risk Officer enforces position limits (10% max), $50 hard cap, and 5% drawdown circuit breaker. Vetoes override everything.
SECURITY ARCHITECTURE

WICK is an agentic bot that only knows how to evaluate and win trades. It reads market data, analyzes signals across 4 specialized desks, and executes orders when the math confirms an edge.

Non-custodial by design. WICK operates via secure, isolated API keys. Your funds never leave your broker account, and WICK has absolutely zero technical permission or capability to withdraw or transfer your capital. You retain 100% custody at all times.

All credentials are AES-256 encrypted at rest. You can revoke access instantly by deleting your API key from your broker's settings panel.
